Gunpla

The Japanese art of building and painting plastic models.

Perhaps one of Japanese most popular franchises, Gundam, has spawned dozens of television shows, video games and movies. However, the most unexpected product of the Gundam universe is a hobby not widely known outside of Asia: Gunpla. Look around to get to know this hobby.
